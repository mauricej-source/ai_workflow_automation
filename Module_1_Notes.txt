Current situation: 
-- --------------------------------------------
The Swift Supply Co customer service team is overwhelmed, leading to delayed responses, frustrated customers, and lost business. Agents spend over 40% of their time handling repetitive inquiries, such as order status and product availability instead of focusing on complex customer needs.

1.  Inquiry Received (1-2 minutes)
     - A customer submits a question through email, phone, live chat, or a web form.
	 
2.  Inquiry Routed (5-10 minutes)
    - A team member manually checks the inbox or platform and assigns the inquiry to an available agent.
	
3.  Agent Reviews Request (3-5 minutes)
    - The assigned agent reads the message and determines the type of inquiry (order status, return request, product availability, complaint, etc.).
	
4.  Agent Information Search by Order Number (5-12 minutes)
    - The agent looks up information across multiple systems:
    - Order tracking system
    - Inventory system
    - Policy documentation
    - This often requires switching between tabs, copying/pasting order numbers, or checking with other departments.
	
5.  Response Drafted & Sent (5-8 minutes)
    - The agent writes a custom reply, often using boilerplate responses or templates. They may manually attach tracking links or summarize 
    - product availability.
	
6.  If complex, ESCALATE (varies: 3-10+ minutes)
    - If the request is complex (e.g., complaint, return issue), the agent forwards it to a supervisor or escalations team via email or phone.
